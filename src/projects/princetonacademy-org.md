details: This was the first website that I have ever built. I was in the right place at the right time. Jeffrey Zeldman's book, Designing with Web Standards, had just come out and I was both a parent and a volunteer at the school. The school was new and had a website that consisted of just a couple of pages. I had heard of MovableType, one of the earliest static site generators, and it fit my mental model of how a website could be constructed as pages via a content management system. In addition to learning from Zeldman's book, the very first An Event Apart conference was held in Philadelphia in 2005, just an hour away from Princeton. I attended and received feedback on the site from Zeldman as well as Eric Meyer and Jason Santa Maria. I was very fortunate and building this site gave me a great introduction to web development and set me on the path to building more websites over the years. The site has since been redesigned as the school has grown.
